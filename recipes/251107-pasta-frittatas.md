<h3>Ingredients:</h3>
<ul>
<li>2x 300g Pasta Salad</li>
<li>100g finely diced ham</li>
<li>2 eggs, whisked</li>
<li>1 cup grated tasty cheese</li>
<li>1/3 cup roughly chopped Continental parsley</li>
<li>Sea salt flakes and ground white pepper</li>
<li>6 cherry truss tomatoes</li>
</ul>
</div>
            
<div class="column last">
<h3>Method:</h3>
<ol>
<li>Preheat oven to 200C. Grease and line the base of 6 x Texas muffin moulds with baking paper. Combine pasta salad, ham, eggs, Â½ cup tasty cheese, parsley, salt and pepper in a large bowl.</li>
<li>Spoon pasta salad mixture into prepared muffin moulds. Sprinkle remaining cheese onto frittatas. Place a tomato on top of each frittata. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden and cooked through. Serve.</li>
</ol>
