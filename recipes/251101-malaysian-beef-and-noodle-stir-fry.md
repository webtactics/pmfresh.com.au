<div class="column">
                <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
<ul>
<li>½ cup crunchy peanut butter</li>
<li>½ cup hot water</li>
<li>2 tbs hoisin sauce</li>
<li>From Good Roots Crunchy Salad Kit 520gm</li>
<li>200g rice stick noodles</li>
<li>1 tbs vegetable oil</li>
<li>500g beef fillet, thinly sliced diagonally</li>
<li>1 long red chilli, thinly sliced diagonally</li>
<li>1/3 cup fried shallots, to serve</li>
<li>1 cup coriander sprigs, to serve</li>
</ul>
            </div>
            
<div class="column last">
            <h3>Method:</h3>
<ol>
<li>Whisk peanut butter, water, hoisin sauce and sauce sachet from salad in a jug. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes or until softened. Drain.</li>
<li>Heat 2 tsp oil in a wok over a high heat. Cook beef in batches, adding more oil as required. Add salad from salad mix to wok and stir fry for 2 minutes. Return beef to wok with noodles and sauce. Stir fry for 2 minutes or until heated through. Serve topped with sliced red chilli, crisp noodles from kit, fried shallots and coriander sprigs.</li>
</ol>
            </div>
